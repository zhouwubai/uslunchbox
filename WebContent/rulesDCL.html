To all FIU students and scholars:

The knowledge discovery research group in the School of Computing and Information Sciences at Florida International University (FIU) and South Garden Chinese Restaurant (arguably the best Chinese Restaurant in Miami) launch USLunchBox (www.uslunchbox.com) with the goal of providing FIU students and scholars with convenient and discounted online ordering of authentic Chinese food:

Save Time and Money/Convenient Online Ordering
  • Use your valid FIU email address to place the orders online.
  • South Garden Chinese Restaurant deliver the food on campus at GC center, twice a day (lunch & dinner).
  • The healthy, delicious meal includes vegetables and meat of your choice, flat $5!

Free Membership: Become A Member, Receive More Benefits
  • Credits will be accumulated to your account: get 1 free lunchbox for every 10 orders.

Dine-In With Great Discounts, Get The SUN DINING Card
  • Card benefits include convenient membership services with best deals and discounts (10% OFF) when dine-in at local restaurants (Email us at uslunchbox@gmail.com to apply, it's simple).

If you have any concerns or questions, please email us at uslunchbox@gmail.com.
